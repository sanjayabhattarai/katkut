Why we built Katkut AI

A day of filming shouldn't turn into an hour of editing before you can post it.

If you've ever shot 40 short clips on a trip, a shoot day, or just a normal Tuesday, you know the problem: the footage is good, but turning it into something postable means scrubbing through every clip, guessing which ten seconds are actually worth keeping, and manually stitching it all into a vertical video before you even get to the fun part: captions, music, transitions.

Katkut AI exists to skip straight to the fun part. Drop your clips in, and it does the unglamorous work automatically: throws out the blurry and dark ones, finds the sharpest moment in each clip, decides which clips deserve their original sound, and assembles it all into a clean 9:16 rough cut. Then it hands off to CapCut, TikTok, or Instagram for you to add the voice, the music, the captions. We don't try to be a full editor. That's deliberate.

How it works

  1. Pick your clips. Select the raw videos (and photos) straight from your camera roll. No import step, no upload. Katkut AI works best with 4 or more clips.
  2. Choose a style. Auto, Travel & Adventure, Mini Vlog, Food & Cooking, or Unboxing. Each paces cuts differently for the kind of footage it's tuned for.
  3. Set a length. Anywhere from under 30 seconds to over two minutes, plus whether to let Katkut AI decide per-clip audio or mute everything for your own voiceover.
  4. AI builds the rough cut. On-device analysis scores every clip for sharpness, exposure, and motion, then assembles the best moments into one sequenced, 9:16 reel, in seconds, not scrubbing.
  5. Fine-tune and export. Trim, reorder, or mute clips in the timeline if you want changes, then export in Standard HD or Mastering Quality and share straight to TikTok, CapCut, or Instagram.

Frequently asked questions

What is Katkut AI?

Katkut AI is a mobile app that turns a pile of raw video clips, whether it's 10, 20, 50, or more, into a ready-to-post 9:16 vertical reel automatically. Pick your clips straight from your camera roll (nothing is ever uploaded, every bit of analysis and editing happens on your phone), and Katkut AI finds the best moments in each one, trims the rest, and arranges everything into a smooth sequence in seconds.

No editing experience? No problem. Katkut AI is built for two kinds of users:

  • Professional editors and content creators: it's your AI pre-editor. Instead of starting from scratch, Katkut AI hands you a strong first cut, saving hours of repetitive work so you can focus on the creative finishing touches.
  • Everyone else: it's the easiest way to turn raw footage into something worth posting. If you've never edited a video before, Katkut AI does the hard work for you.

Katkut AI deliberately stops at the rough cut, that's by design. Add your own voiceover, music, and captions afterward in CapCut, TikTok, or Instagram, then export and share straight to your favorite platform.

Does Katkut AI upload my clips anywhere?

No. Every clip is analyzed and every reel is assembled entirely on your phone. Nothing is ever uploaded to a server. That's a hard rule of how the app is built, not just a privacy policy promise.

Is Katkut AI a replacement for CapCut or a full video editor?

No, and that's deliberate. Katkut AI stops at the rough cut: trimmed, sequenced, and muted where it should be. Voiceover, music, and captions still get added afterward in whichever app you already use to finish, like CapCut, TikTok, or Instagram.

How does the AI decide which moments to keep?

It measures every clip for sharpness, exposure, motion/freeze frames, and audio energy, then keeps the sharpest, best-lit, steadiest segment from each one and skips blurry, dark, or shaky footage automatically. The style you pick (Travel, Food, Mini Vlog, etc.) shapes the pacing of how those moments get cut together.

What if some of my clips are landscape or square, not vertical?

They're never cropped harshly and never get black bars. Katkut AI centers the original footage and fills the empty space with a blurred, enlarged copy of the same clip, so the output is always a clean 9:16 vertical reel.

How many clips do I need, and how long can the reel be?

At least 4 clips is recommended for the best results. Most people drop in around 40 short clips shot across a day, trip, or shoot. Target reel length ranges from under 30 seconds up to over 2 minutes; Katkut AI fits the best material to whichever length you choose.

What happens to the original audio?

By default, Smart audio decides per clip: it keeps sound where it's actually part of the moment (talking, music, cheering) and mutes the rest. You can also mute every clip up front if you're planning to add your own voiceover or music track instead.

Yes, the free tier includes AI-picked rough cuts and every style preset, with a watermark on exports. Katkut AI Pro ($3.99/month) removes the watermark and unlocks unlimited reels. See Pricing for details.
