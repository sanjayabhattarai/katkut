Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Katkut AI ("Katkut", "we", "us") is a mobile app that helps you turn short video clips and photos into a ready-to-post reel. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

The short version: your video clips and photos are processed entirely on your phone and are never uploaded to our servers. The only personal information we collect is what you share by signing in with Google, used solely to identify your account.

1. Information we collect

Account information (only if you sign in). Signing in with Google is optional. If you choose to sign in, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account via our authentication provider (Supabase), so we can identify your account and, in the future, your subscription status.

Your media — processed on-device only. When you pick videos or photos to build a reel, Katkut reads them locally on your device to analyze, trim, and assemble your reel. These files are never uploaded to any server — all analysis, editing, and export happen entirely on your phone.

Exported reel. The finished reel is saved to your device's gallery so you can share it yourself (e.g. to TikTok, Instagram, or CapCut). We do not receive a copy of it.

2. Permissions we request, and why

  • Photos/Videos access — to let you pick clips and build your reel. Clips stay on your device and are never uploaded.
  • Save to gallery — to save your finished reel back to your device.

3. How we use information

  • To operate your account and verify your subscription status (free vs. Pro).
  • To show ads to free-tier users, and to remove ads and the watermark for Pro subscribers.
  • To provide customer support if you contact us.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Advertising

Katkut is free to use, supported by ads shown to free-tier users (alongside a watermark on exported reels). Subscribing to Pro removes both. Ads are delivered by third-party advertising networks, which may collect information such as your device's advertising identifier, approximate location, and app usage data to deliver and measure ads, including personalized ads. You can generally limit ad personalization through your device's ad settings (for example, "Opt out of Ads Personalization" in your Android privacy settings).

This section will be updated with the specific ad network(s) we use and links to their privacy policies once integrated.

5. Third-party services

We use Supabase to handle account sign-in and store basic account information (name, email, profile photo). We do not currently use any analytics SDKs. See the "Advertising" section above for our ad network disclosures.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your reels, drafts, and picked media stay on your device and are removed when you delete the app or the project itself. If you signed in, you can request deletion of your account information at any time by contacting us (below).

7. Children's privacy

Katkut is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do.
